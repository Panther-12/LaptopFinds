Our Key Selling Point: The Offer System

The heart of our platform lies in our offer system, a groundbreaking feature that sets us apart from traditional online retailers. We understand that every student's budget is different, and our offer system allows students to propose a price they are willing to pay for a laptop tagged with the "bargain" label. This way, students have the opportunity to get their desired laptops at a price that aligns with their financial constraints.

How It Works

Bargain Tag

Only laptops marked with the "bargain" tag are eligible for the offer system. These laptops are carefully curated to ensure they meet our quality standards while still offering significant discounts.

Propose Your Price

Students can browse through our extensive collection of laptops and select the ones with the bargain tag that catch their eye. Once they find a laptop they wish to purchase, they can propose a price within their budget using our user-friendly offer system.

Vendor Decision

After a student submits their offer, our platform securely forwards it to the vendor. The vendor then has the option to either accept or decline the proposed price. If the vendor accepts the offer, the laptop is sold at the agreed-upon price.

Negotiation

In some cases, the vendor may counter the student's offer with a slightly higher price. This opens up a negotiation opportunity, allowing students and vendors to find a mutually beneficial price point
